Privacy Policy for AllCodeRelay

Last updated: May 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how AllCodeRelay ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

When you use AllCodeRelay, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Scanned Data: Information from QR codes, barcodes, and NFC tags that you scan using the App.
  • Webhook URLs: URLs that you configure for sending scanned data.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device, such as device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use the App, including crash reports and performance data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Send scanned data to your configured webhook endpoints
  • Improve and optimize the App's performance
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you configure a webhook URL, scanned data will be sent to that endpoint.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as hosting and analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Webhook URLs are stored securely using platform-specific secure storage mechanisms.
  • Scanned data is temporarily stored on your device and is only transmitted to your configured webhook endpoints.
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access and delete locally stored scan history
  • Configure or remove webhook endpoints
  • Disable features like automatic clipboard copying
  • Uninstall the App at any time

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from your child, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
